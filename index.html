What is wine-investing with NFT?

The French wine-investing industry tends to be private and based on long-standing relations between the château and merchants. Before there was no possibility to invest in the premium Grand Cru Classe wine segment using a bottle measure.

Getting access to buy Grand Cru Classe en primeur wine is the privilege of intermediates like importers, wine shops and wholesalers. Moreover, the price gets a mark-up of taxes, transport costs etc. Winessy aims to allow wine admirers, vintage wine collectors from all over the world to start investing from 1 bottle and get their opportunity to enjoy earning on it. Due to the limits of the allowable production of a particular wine, the exclusivity of vintage wine may keep on growing up to 15% per year while the quantity of bottles decreases.
